Introduction

Before winter break in December of my sophomore year at Colby, my friend approached me with the spontaneous idea of running a marathon. I was no distance runner, so I was quite hesitant initially. After mulling the idea over, though, I realized it was a perfect opportunity to take up the challenge. My goal of joining the boys' varsity soccer team recently fell through due to repeatedly pulling my hamstring during the summer leading up to tryouts. I had a baseline in cardio from my four-plus years on my high school rowing team, and now that I had no personal fitness goals, my friend's idea of running a marathon actually didn’t seem like such a crazed, impulsive idea after all. While running is a hobby for me, so is data science. I thought, why not take these passions of mine and run some numbers? Here is my data-driven report of my marathon, using Garmin’s API data, which I cleaned with R and Pandas, and plotted using Python’s Plotly library.
